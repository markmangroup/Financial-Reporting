## The Features Mike-OS Actually Needs

### 1. The "You're Stuck" Detector

**What it does**:
- Tracks how many days since you've done "real work" (not coding your own tools)
- Tracks how many days since you've applied to jobs
- Tracks how many days since you've reached out to anyone
- When you cross threshold (3 days? 7 days?), it gets LOUD

**UI**:
```
üö® STUCK ALERT üö®

It's been 12 days since you applied to a job.
It's been 8 days since you reached out to anyone in your network.
It's been 5 days since you did client work.

You're building tools instead of doing the hard thing.

Click here to see your "Avoid List" ‚Üí
```

### 2. The "Avoid List"

**What it is**:
- The things you know you should do but haven't
- Each item shows: days since added, days since touched, why you're avoiding it

**Example**:
```
LinkedIn Profile Update
Added: 23 days ago
Last touched: Never
Why avoiding: "Feels performative, don't know what to say"
Smallest next step: Write one bullet point about your skills

‚Üí DO THIS NOW (takes 5 min)
```

### 3. The "Small Wins" Tracker

**What it does**:
- Forces you to log ONE small win per day
- If you don't log by 8pm, it bugs you
- Shows streak (like Duolingo guilt)

**Why this matters**:
When you're stuck, ANY forward movement helps. Even tiny wins.

### 4. The "Network Nudge"

**What it does**:
- Reminds you to reach out to 1 person per week
- Suggests WHO (based on your contacts)
- Provides templates (so you don't have to think)

**Example**:
```
It's been 11 days since you reached out to anyone.

Suggested reach-out:
‚Üí John at KKR (last spoke 4 months ago)

Template:
"Hey John, hope you're well. I've been exploring what's next after
leaving IQVIA - curious if you know of any companies looking for
finance/data leadership. Would love to catch up if you have 15 min."

[Send via LinkedIn] [Mark as Done] [Skip for now]
```

### 5. The "Reality Check" Dashboard

**What it shows**:
- Financial runway (months until you need income)
- Days since last client work
- Days since last job application
- Days since last interview
- Current "stuck score" (based on above metrics)

**Why this matters**:
Numbers don't lie. Seeing "98 days since last application" is a kick in the ass.

### 6. The "What You're Actually Working On" Log

**What it does**:
- Every time you open VSCode, logs what project
- At end of day, shows: "You spent 6 hours coding Mike-OS, 0 hours on anything that makes money"
- Asks: "Was this time well spent?"

**Why this matters**:
Makes you confront whether you're working or avoiding.

### 7. The "Job Application Assistant"

**What it does**:
- Scrapes LinkedIn/Indeed for roles matching your profile
- Shows 3 new roles every morning
- One-click apply with customized cover letter (AI-generated)
- Tracks: applied, interviewed, rejected, offer

**Why this matters**:
Removes friction. You just have to click "Apply" not "find jobs, write cover letters, track applications."

### 8. The "Dependency Alarm"

**What it tracks**:
- How much you're talking to AI (me, ChatGPT, etc.)
- How much you're talking to humans
- When AI time >> human time, it warns you

**Example alert**:
```

- AI conversations: 18 hours
- Human conversations: 2 hours
```
